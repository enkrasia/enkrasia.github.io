**The Logical Structure of Argument, in General**
1. (TA): $\Box \forall x (Fx)$
2. (IS): $GX$
3. (PP)$\Diamond \exists x(Gx)$
4. (CP)$\exists x(Gx) \Box \rightarrow \forall x(Gx \rightarrow \neg Fx)$
